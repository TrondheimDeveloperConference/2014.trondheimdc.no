Audience fragmentation, the increasing use of digital technologies beyond the screen and the high level of competition in the digital landscape offers tremendous opportunities and also relevant challenges to anyone trying to make it in this space. The talk will explore how Google is looking to support developers, both technically and from a business perspective, in their strive to develop for the world of tomorrow.</p>
<h4>STEFANIA MONTAGNA:</h4><p>Stefania Montagna is a Strategic Partner Manager in Google&#8217;s Online Partnership Group, focusing on the Nordics. Stefaniaspecializes in sales of revenue-generating solutions for publishers and app developers. She has previously contributed to maintaining the Google Blog &#8220;Inside AdWords&#8221; for Norway, and has organized several Google events in Norway and Sweden.</p>
