In order to have more creative productivity, we need to hack how we use our brains on a daily basis in order to access more of its inherent processing and creative power. Through exploring various concepts and approaches, including the neuroscience of creativity, productivity techniques, and emerging practices that spur innovation, we&#8217;ll discover not only the ways in which our brains work best, but also what’s behind the times when we feel on fire with creativity and when we don&#8217;t. <h4>DENISE JACOBS:</h4><p>Denise R. Jacobs is a Speaker + Writer + Creativity Evangelist who teaches techniques to make the creative process more fluid, methods for making work environments more conducive to creative productivity, and practices for sparking innovation.
