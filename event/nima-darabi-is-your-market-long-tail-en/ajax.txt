(EN):</h2><h4>Klokken 16:30, spor 4</h4><p>Distribution of online content consumption is claimed to be “long-tail” rather than “top-heavy”, however we believe there are many exceptions. We propose a statistical measure that tells where digital markets behave differently; whether few blockbusters in the “head” are dominating or a long list of niche products in the “tail” will cumulatively take up a considerable share.</p>
<h4>NIMA DARABI:</h4><p>An enthusiastic data scientist at Zedge with experience on social, bio and neuro data. Aiming at discovering common characteristics behind various data sets and viral phenomena in different complex networks. Lately interested in use of big data analytics more effectively in the design of products, user experience, and marketing.</p>
