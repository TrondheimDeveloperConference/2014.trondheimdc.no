<a class="pops-exit" href="http://2014.trondheimdc.no/">&times;</a><img width="400" height="400" src="http://2014.trondheimdc.no/wp-content/uploads/2014/08/Sirar_Salih-400x400.jpg" class="pops-face wp-post-image" alt="Sirar Salih" /><h2> TDD with AngularJS and TypeScript (EN):</h2><h4>Klokken 12:30, spor 2</h4><p>Writing clean, testable JavaScript can be a daunting task for front-end developers. Many find it difficult to get into and thus discard it. Test automation is an essential part of modern web applications, especially when it comes to maintainability. In this talk, I show how easy and straightforward testable JS code can be written. Practicing TDD and combining the power of AngularJS and TypeScript.</p>
<h4>SIRAR SALIH:</h4><p>Sirar Salih is an experienced consultant working at Itera Norway. He is VP at the Norwegian .NET User Group (NNUG) and board member of the Oslo chapter. He is also board member of the AngularJS Oslo user group. Sirar has experience from speaking at international conferences such as the Norwegian Developers Conference (NDC), HTML5 Developer Conference and various community user groups.</p>
